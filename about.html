My Story

[(Thus Far)

Yuanda Zhang is a senior at Sentinel Secondary in West Vancouver, where his work sits at the intersection of engineering, sailing, and leadership. He has represented Canada at the ILCA 4 Youth World Championships and earned gold at the BC Summer Games, building a competitive record shaped by precision and decision-making under pressure. Alongside competition, Yuanda serves as a Chief Petty Officer in the Royal Canadian Sea Cadets, where he has been named Sailor of the Year three consecutive times and selected for national-level instructor training. He has led cadets through on-water programs and contributed to training operations at HMCS Quadra. His engineering work focuses on turning real-world observations into systems. He designed a sailing performance device that logs wind and motion data to improve race decisions, extending the same problem-solving approach he developed as a VEX Robotics Worlds Division Finalist. Beyond competition and engineering, Yuanda has coached over 70 young sailors, mentored robotics teams, and volunteered extensively in community sailing programs.
