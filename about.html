About Me

Designer, developer, and artist exploring the edges where imagination meets technology.

The longer story

I build things that feel alive—interactive websites, immersive visuals, and tools that help people express themselves. My work lives at the intersection of storytelling and systems thinking, borrowing from architecture, motion design, and playful experimentation.

When I’m not behind a screen, you’ll find me sketching scenes from city life, capturing cinematic photographs, or cooking elaborate meals for friends. Everything I do feeds back into the art.

What I’m great at

  • Designing expressive digital interfaces with motion-first thinking.
  • Creative coding across the web—WebGL, canvas, and responsive layouts.
  • Story-driven visuals for music, events, and brands.
  • Rapid prototyping and collaboration with multidisciplinary teams.
