Layers We Leave Behind

At the base, life is simple, close to the ground and the fire. As you look up, each level carries something forward and leaves something behind. The city doesn’t replace what came before it, it stacks on top of it, rising higher, faster, brighter. Progress here isn’t clean or equal, but it keeps climbing anyway.
